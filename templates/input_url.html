Enter URL of Amazon Product

Instructions

  1. Go to www.amazon.in
  2. Enter your choice of product in the search bar and click "Enter"
  3. Click on the product of your choice
  4. Wait for the page to load and copy the URL from the address bar of your browser
  5. Paste the URL to the text box below and click "Extract & Predict"



NOTE: Extracting reviews will take 5-10 minutes,
depending on the number of reviews for the given product
